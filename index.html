Notes Before Posting

Good to share

Project screenshots, code notes, astronomy facts, and what changed in a game level.

Ask first

Photos, real schedules, exact locations, school details, or anything about other people.

Never share

Passwords, recovery codes, private messages, payment info, or home address details.
